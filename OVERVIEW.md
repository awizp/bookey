# Bookey
  A book platform which is showing all kind of books are seperated by genre

### User types

* Guest
* Login - Novice
* Login - Veteran

### Sample books

Whether there are users novice or veteran they want to look into what they want to do in our website. So, we have to give them samples to read and the overview of the books what they want to read.

Sample books are simply copyright free books which is globally accepted to read, maybe old books and many of the named books fall into this category (Sherlock Holmes, City of Gold, Alice in wonderland, Velpaari)

### Genre

* Fiction - Action, Adventure, Sci Fi, Medivel, Imagine
* Non fiction - History, Biography, Self help, Travels, Cook books
* Comics - American cartoon books, DC, MCU, Dynamic
* Graphic Novels - Novels drawn for children
* Novels - Which is mostly literature books who adults can understand
* Manga - Japanese black & white hand drawn comics
* Manhwa - Coloured korean comics which is hand drawn famous for arts and story

### Dashboard

* user details on top - username, name and readed books count
* user playlist - loved books, want to read, dropped books, reccommended books
* user bio - about themselves and the story about the read journey

### Feed

* user can read and write posts about the books what they are reading
* Posting pictures about what their companions while reading a book
* Can like and dislike the posts which is mostly liked posts can attract the books what they are reading

### Features

* Book clubs - user can make a club and add a party member to read that particular book and attain read streaks
* Genre clubs - users all can make a group and tell a story about the book related to genre
